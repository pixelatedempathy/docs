## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect
at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day pilot program for qualifying organizations. Contact our
sales team to learn more.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all
training data before cancellation.

### Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, Enterprise plans include volume pricing. Contact sales for a custom quote.
