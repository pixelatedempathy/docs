## Reseller Partners

### Become a Reseller

Offer Pixelated Empathy to your clients and earn competitive commissions:

- **Commission Structure**: 15-25% recurring revenue
- **Sales Support**: Dedicated partner manager
- **Marketing Resources**: Co-branded materials
- **Training**: Sales certification program

- Established client base in healthcare or education
- Dedicated sales team
- Ability to provide first-line support
- Commitment to minimum annual sales
