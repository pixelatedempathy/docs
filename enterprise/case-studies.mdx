## University Counseling Program

### Background

A large public university's counseling psychology program needed to provide more
practical training opportunities for graduate students. Traditional role-play
exercises were time-intensive and inconsistent.

### Challenge

- Limited opportunities for students to practice therapeutic techniques
- Inconsistent feedback from different supervisors
- Difficulty exposing students to diverse client scenarios
- Time constraints in the training curriculum

### Solution

The program integrated Pixelated Empathy's Empathy Gym™ into their training
curriculum, allowing students to:

- Practice with 200+ diverse client scenarios
- Receive consistent, AI-powered feedback
- Track progress across competency domains
- Prepare for practicum placements

### Results

| Metric                      | Before | After | Improvement |
| --------------------------- | ------ | ----- | ----------- |
| Trainee Readiness Score     | 62%    | 87%   | +40%        |
| Supervisor Time (hrs/week)  | 15     | 8     | -47%        |
| Student Confidence          | 3.2/5  | 4.5/5 | +41%        |
| Practicum Placement Success | 78%    | 94%   | +21%        |

> _"Pixelated Empathy has transformed how we prepare our students. They arrive
> at their practicum placements with real skills and confidence."_ — Dr. Sarah
> Chen, Director of Clinical Training

## Regional Hospital System

### Background (2)

A regional hospital system with 500 beds needed to train new mental health staff
across multiple facilities. Traditional training was inconsistent and expensive.

### Challenge (2)

- High turnover requiring constant training
- Inconsistent training quality across facilities
- Limited availability of senior staff for supervision
- Compliance requirements for documentation

### Solution (2)

The hospital system deployed Pixelated Empathy across all facilities with:

- Custom scenarios reflecting their patient population
- Integration with their LMS for tracking
- Standardized competency assessments
- 24/7 availability for shift workers

### Results (2)

| Metric                           | Before | After  | Improvement |
| -------------------------------- | ------ | ------ | ----------- |
| Training Time (weeks)            | 12     | 4.8    | -60%        |
| Competency Assessment Pass Rate  | 72%    | 91%    | +26%        |
| Training Cost per Employee       | $4,200 | $1,800 | -57%        |
| Staff Satisfaction with Training | 3.1/5  | 4.6/5  | +48%        |

> _"We've cut our training time in half while actually improving outcomes. The
> ROI has been exceptional."_ — Michael Torres, Director of Behavioral Health
> Services

## Community Mental Health Center

### Background (4)

A community mental health center serving a diverse urban population needed to
train staff to work with clients from many different backgrounds.

### Challenge (4)

- Staff needed cultural competency training
- High caseloads limited training time
- Diverse client population with varied needs
- Limited budget for professional development

### Solution (4)

The center used Pixelated Empathy's cultural empathy modules:

- Scenarios featuring diverse client backgrounds
- Bias detection and correction training
- Language-specific cultural considerations
- Affordable pricing for non-profits

### Results (4)

| Metric                                  | Before | After | Improvement |
