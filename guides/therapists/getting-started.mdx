## Account Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept Your Invitation">
    Click the enrollment link in your invitation email. This takes you to the
    Pixelated Empathy registration page with your institution pre-selected.

    If you don't have an invitation link, contact your supervisor or email
    **support@pixelatedempathy.com** to request access.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Credentials">
    Enter your email address and create a strong password. We enforce the
    following requirements:

    - Minimum 12 characters
    - At least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character
    - Cannot match your email address or common dictionary words

    <Tip>
      We support **two-factor authentication (2FA)** and strongly recommend
      enabling it during setup. You can use any TOTP-compatible authenticator
      app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, etc.).
    </Tip>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.
    The link expires after 24 hours.

    <Info>
      Didn't receive the email? Check your spam folder, or click **Resend
      Verification** on the login page. If issues persist, contact support.
    </Info>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Institutional Agreement">
    Review and accept the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and your
    institution's data handling agreement. These documents outline how your
    training data is protected and used.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Learning Goals

After your first session, we recommend setting structured learning goals to
guide your training journey.

### Creating Effective Goals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Weekly Practice Goals" icon="calendar-week">
    Set a target for how many sessions you want to complete each week. We recommend:

    - **Beginners**: 2â€“3 sessions per week
    - **Intermediate**: 3â€“5 sessions per week
    - **Advanced**: 1â€“2 focused sessions per week on specific skill gaps

    Consistency matters more than volume. Three thoughtful sessions with full
    review are more valuable than ten rushed ones.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Skill-Specific Goals" icon="bullseye">
    Target specific competency scores you want to achieve. For example:

    - "Improve my empathy score from 0.65 to 0.80 within 4 weeks"
    - "Complete 5 crisis intervention scenarios with zero missed crisis signals"
    - "Reduce bias detection alerts by 50% over the next month"

    The platform tracks these goals automatically and sends you progress
    updates.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reflection Goals" icon="book-open">
    Build a habit of self-reflection after each session:

    - Write at least 3 sentences of personal reflection per session
    - Identify one thing you did well and one area for growth
    - Track recurring patterns in your feedback over time

    The **Session Review** page includes guided reflection prompts to help you
    get started.

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
